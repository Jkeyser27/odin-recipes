Delicous Lasagna Recipe

This is my family recipe that we make every Sunday. This lasagna is a favorite and is incredibly easy to make. It has simple ingredients but tons of flavor. This is a great freezer meal, make-ahead recipe, and leftovers freeze and reheat super well.

Ingredients

Cheese Filling

  • 2 cups Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper

Meat Sauce

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 1/4 lb. ground beef
  • 3/4 lb. ground italian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 40 oz. marinara sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire Sauce

Other ingredients and equipment

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2.5 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1 9x13-inch casserole dish

Steps

  1. Combine cheese filling ingredients, set aside
  2. To make the meat sauce, heat olive oil over medium heat and add the diced onions. Soften onions for 10 minutes.
  3. Add beef and sausage and increase heat. Cook for 8-10 minutes.
  4. Add chicken broth, marinara suace, tomato paste, and Worcesterchire sauce. Bring to boil and reduce to a simmer.
  5. Boil pasta noodles and preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  6. Cook noodles to al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  7. Lay out cold noodles on parchment paper.
  8. Begin assembling lasagna by spreading 1 cup of sauce to the bottom of your casserole dish.
  9. Next, add 4 lasagna noodles
  10. Spread 1/3 cup of the cheese filling over noodles followed by 1 1/2 cups meat sauce.
  11. Repeat and top with 2 1/2 cups of Mozzarella cheese.
  12. Spray a piece of foil with nonstick cooking spray and place it over the lasagna
  13. Bake for 35 minutes, remove cover and bake for 10 more minutes.
  14. Remove from oven and let it sit for 15 minutes.
