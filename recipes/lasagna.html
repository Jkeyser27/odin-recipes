Delicous Lasagna Recipe

This is my family recipe that we make every Sunday. This lasagna is a favorite and is incredibly easy to make. It has simple ingredients but tons of flavor. This is a great freezer meal, make-ahead recipe, and leftovers freeze and reheat super well.

Ingredients

Cheese Filling

  • 2 cups Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper

Meat Sauce

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 1/4 lb. ground beef
  • 3/4 lb. ground italian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 40 oz. marinara sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire Sauce

Other ingredients and equipment

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2.5 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1 9x13-inch casserole dish
